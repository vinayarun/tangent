Introducing Tangent – AI That Helps You Think, Not Just Answer

Luxembourg — February 2026

Today we announce the launch of Tangent, a next-generation AI research workspace designed to transform how people explore complex ideas.

Generative AI has dramatically accelerated access to knowledge, but most AI interfaces still rely on linear chat — a format that does not reflect how humans naturally think. When people research deeply, they branch into related questions, test competing ideas, revisit earlier insights, and gradually synthesize understanding.

Tangent introduces a new category of AI experience: conversations that automatically evolve into structured knowledge systems.

Instead of replacing familiar chat interfaces, Tangent enhances them with optional cognitive views that organize conversations as they happen, helping users understand complex topics faster, more deeply, and with greater confidence.

The Problem: AI Conversations Don’t Scale With Complexity

As conversations with AI grow longer and deeper, users frequently experience:

Losing track of key insights buried in transcripts

Difficulty returning to earlier reasoning paths

Tangential explorations disrupting main research threads

Challenges synthesizing conclusions across discussions

Reduced confidence in AI-generated conclusions

Despite using advanced AI, many professionals still rely on notebooks, whiteboards, and manual note-taking to structure their thinking.

Tangent was built to remove this friction.

The Tangent Solution: AI Conversations With Built-In Thinking Structure

Tangent preserves the simplicity of traditional chat while adding optional cognitive workspaces that automatically organize knowledge in real time.

Users can chat naturally with any AI model while Tangent continuously builds a living map of their understanding.

❓ FAQs
Q: What is Tangent?

Tangent is an AI conversation workspace that transforms chats into structured knowledge maps to support deeper thinking and research.

Q: Why is it called Tangent?

The name reflects how human thinking naturally explores related ideas before returning to core understanding. Tangent embraces this process rather than restricting it.

Q: How is Tangent different from AI chat tools?

Most AI tools present conversations as linear transcripts. Tangent augments chat with optional cognitive workspaces that structure understanding automatically.

Q: What is the Tangent Graph?

The Tangent Graph visually represents conversational branches and related ideas, allowing users to explore tangents and return to main discussions effortlessly.

Q: What is the Research Compass?

The Research Compass organizes conversations into stages of structured investigation, helping users maintain clarity and direction when exploring complex topics.

Q: What is the Reasoning Graph?

The Reasoning Graph shows how conclusions were formed by mapping claims, supporting evidence, counterarguments, and unresolved questions.

Q: What is Insight Compression?

Insight Compression automatically synthesizes conversations into layered summaries that allow users to quickly review or deeply explore understanding.

Q: Does Tangent replace AI models?

No. Tangent integrates with multiple AI providers and allows users to bring their own API keys.

Q: Who is Tangent designed for?

Tangent is designed for people performing deep learning, research, analysis, and strategic thinking where reasoning quality and understanding are critical.

Q: Does Tangent require manual organization?

No. Tangent automatically builds structured knowledge views while users chat.

Q: How does Tangent handle AI misunderstandings?

Users can naturally clarify or correct misunderstandings through conversation. Tangent continuously updates its structured knowledge layer accordingly.

Q: Does Tangent store entire conversations?

Yes.

Tangent securely stores full conversation transcripts locally, along with structured knowledge maps and summaries.

When interacting with AI models, Tangent intelligently sends compressed summaries and relevant context to improve efficiency, reduce cost, and maintain focus.

This allows Tangent to preserve complete knowledge history while ensuring fast and efficient AI interactions.
