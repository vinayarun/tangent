# Architecture Decision Records

---

## ADR-001: Local-First Knowledge Storage

Tangent stores full transcripts and knowledge graphs locally.

Reason:
- Privacy
- Reliability
- Long-term knowledge retention

---

## ADR-002: Plugin-Based Visualization System

All cognitive views must be implemented as plugins.

Reason:
- Extensibility
- UI independence
- Open-source ecosystem growth

---

## ADR-003: Knowledge Graph as Core Asset

The knowledge graph is treated as the primary structured representation of conversation understanding.

Reason:
- Enables advanced reasoning
- Supports multi-view visualizations
