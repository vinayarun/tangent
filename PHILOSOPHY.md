# Tangent Philosophy

## Thinking Should Be Structured

Human understanding emerges through:

- Exploration
- Hypothesis testing
- Synthesis
- Revision

---

## System 1 / System 2 Thinking

Tangent supports:

- Linear logical reasoning (System 2)
- Associative exploration (System 1)

---

## External Cognition

Tangent extends working memory by externalizing structured knowledge.

---

## Knowledge Over Conversation

Tangent is designed to transform transient conversations into persistent understanding.
